Aloo Phujia

Description

Aloo Phujia is a delicious North-Indian recipe which is prepared with potatoes, tomatoes, green peas and spices. This vegetarian dish is easy to cook and has mild spicy flavours. If you love potatoes, this simple traditional Indian recipe is sure to win your heart. Serve it with freshly made chapatis and onion and tomato raita or with steamed rice and salad and you are going to have the best ever experience of homemade food. If you want to add some more taste to Aloo Phujia, you can add a few drops of warm ghee to it before serving. It is different from the Aloo Bhujia, the popular dish of Bihar and UP, which is a dry preparation.

Ingredients

    1 chopped onion
    1/4 vegetable oil
    1 pound potatoes, peeled and cubed
    1 teaspoon salt
    1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
    1/2 teaspoon ground tumeric
    1/4 teaspoon cumin
    2 chopped tomatoes

Steps

    Step 1
    Lightly brown onion in oil in a medium size skillet.
    Step 2
    Stir in salt, cayenne, turmeric and cumin. Add potatoes and cook 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
    Step 3
    Add tomatoes, cover pan and cook until potatoes are soft, about 10 minutes.
